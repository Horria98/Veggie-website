Vegan Chocolate Donuts

Prep: 75 mins
Cook: 15-20 mins
Serv: 10 Donuts (each 155cal/4P/2oc/6F)

  1. Peel the banana and mash it. Then mix it with maple syrup and hazelnut butter.
  2. Add the milk and vanilla extract and mix.
  3. Add in the dry ingredients and mix.
  4. Fill the batter in the donut molds.
  5. Bake at 175C-350F for 15-20mins.
  6. For glaze, Melt half a cup of chocolate and then mix it with 1 tbsp of hazelnut butter.

Preparation steps:

Advanced

Ingredients
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup (75g) maple syrup
  • 3 Tbsp hazelnut butter
  • 1 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup + 2 Tbsp (155ml) plant milk
  • 1 cup (125g) flour
  • 1 heaped Tsp baking powder
  • 2 Tbsp cocoa powder
Glaze:
  • 1/2 cup (75g) chocolate
  • 1 Tbsp hazelnut butter
