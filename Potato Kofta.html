Potato Kofta

Prep: 25 mins
Cook: 30-35 mins
Serv: 4 Servings

  1. Bring a pot and fill it with water, and leave it on the fire until the water boils
  2. Add the potatoes to the pot, and leave them in the water until they are boiled
  3. Remove the potatoes from the boiling water and leave them in the colander until they are very drained and cool
  4. Put the potatoes in a bowl and mash them using a fork
  5. Add the egg yolk, flour, green onion, parsley, green coriander, dill and garlic, to the potatoes, and stir until all are mixed.
  6. Season the potato mixture with salt, black pepper, turmeric and coriander and keep stirring
  7. Potato bread in the form of medium-sized kofta fingers
  8. Take a frying pan, put the oil in it, and leave it on the fire until the oil gets hot
  9. Cover the potato kofta slices with flour, then put them in the oil, stirring until they are golden brown, then remove them on kitchen paper and serve.

For Begginers

Ingredients
  • 2 large size potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon (teaspoon) flour
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons (tbsp) chopped coriander
  • 2 tablespoons (tablespoon)
  • 2 sticks chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic mashed
  • 2 teaspoons (teaspoons) dried coriander, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon (teaspoon) salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon (teaspoon) turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon (tea) ground black pepper
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 large cup of flour
