Vegan Chocolate Cake

Prep: 20 mins
Cook:25-30 mins
Serv: ( 12 pieces each 255cal / 4P / 33C / 10F )

  1. Combine dry ingredients toghether.
  2. Stir in the wet ingredients and mix well.
  3. Stir in the chocolate mix and then the dry ingredients.
  4. Bake at 175C-350F for 25-30mins.
  5. Let cool down completely and add the frosting then enjoy.

Preparation steps:

Intermediate

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups ( 180g ) flour
  • 3 Tbsp cocoa powder
  • 5/8 cup ( 120g ) sugar
  • 1 1/2 Tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup ( 240ml ) plant milk
  • 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar or lemon juice
  • 50g melted vegan butter
Frosting :
  • 1/2 cup (130g) agave or maple syrup
  • 3/4 cup (90g) cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup (50g) melted coconut oil
  • OPTIONAL Smacktastic choco
