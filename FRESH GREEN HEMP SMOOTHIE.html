Fresh Green Hemp Smoothie

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: blender
Serv: 1 Servings

  1. Place all ingredients in blender and blend to desired consistency.
  2. Add more water as needed.
  3. Your smoothie is ready. enjoy!

For Begginers

Ingredients
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 good size mandarin or 2 small cuties (or ½ – ¾ cup fresh berries)
  • handful spinach
  • 2 tablespoons hemp hearts (hulled hemp seeds) or 3 Tbsp hemp protein powder
  • ½ cup purified water + more as needed
  • 4 ice cubes
