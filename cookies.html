Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep: 10 mins
REFRIGERATE 1h
Cook: 10-12 mins
Serv: 14 cookies (each 192cal / 2P / 26C9F )

  1. Mix the ingredients into cookie dough.
  2. Make about 14 cookie dough balls.
  3. Refrigerate for 1h. It's important to refrigerate the cookie dough balls for 1h, otherwise they'll spread too wide when baking.
  4. Bake at 180C-360F for 10-12mins.

Preparation steps:

Intermediate

Ingredients
  • 1/3 cup ( 70g ) white sugar
  • 1/2 cup ( 120g ) brown sugar
  • 110g softened vegan butter or oil
  • 4 Tbsp plant milk
  • 1 3/4 cup ( 220g ) flour
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 Tsp baking powder
  • vegan chocolate chunks
