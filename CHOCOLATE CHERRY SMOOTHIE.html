Chocolate Cherry Smoothie

Prep: 5 mins
Cook: blender
Serv: 2 Servings

  1. In blender, add ingredients and blend until smooth, adding a little extra milk as needed.
  2. Your smoothie is ready. enjoy!

For Begginers

Ingredients
  • 2 cups frozen cherries
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (plain or vanilla)
  • 2 tablespoons raw cacao powder (cocoa powder is ok too)
  • 1 small medjool date (pitted and chopped) or 1 teaspoon pure maple syrup
  • 1 scoop protein powder (plain or vanilla) or 2 tablespoons almond butter
